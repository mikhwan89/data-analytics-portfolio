CAMP

Built Bitcoin Backtest Simulation Dashboard for Investment Clients

Developed an interactive simulation dashboard that lets clients model real investment scenarios with Bitcoin. Clients can specify any entry and exit date to see historical return outcomes, and separately simulate the effect of adding a 1%, 5%, or 10% Bitcoin allocation to their existing portfolio — visualising the impact on both overall return and risk-adjusted metrics including Sharpe ratio, volatility, and max drawdown. Turns complex quantitative analysis into a self-serve tool accessible to non-technical investors.
